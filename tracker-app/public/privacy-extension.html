Chrome Extension

Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

JobTracker - Auto Apply ("the Extension") is a browser extension that helps job seekers discover and apply to jobs. This policy explains what data the Extension accesses and how it is used.

Data We Access

  • LinkedIn session cookie (li_at): Read from your browser to authenticate job searches and Easy Apply submissions on your behalf. This cookie is never sent to any third-party server.
  • Job page content: The Extension reads job listing pages on LinkedIn and ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, etc.) to extract job details and fill application forms.
  • Local storage: The Extension stores your connection status and preferences locally in your browser using chrome.storage.local.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your LinkedIn credentials (email/password).
  • We do not send your session cookie to any external server.
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside of job application pages.
  • We do not sell, share, or monetize any user data.
  • We do not use analytics, telemetry, or tracking scripts in the Extension.

How Data Flows

All communication stays within your browser:

  • The Extension communicates with the JobTracker dashboard (tracker-app-lyart.vercel.app) via in-page messaging (window.postMessage). No data leaves your browser.
  • When you apply to a job, the Extension opens the application page in your browser and fills the form using your profile data that you configured in the dashboard.
  • Application results (success/failure) are stored locally and displayed in your dashboard.

Permissions Explained

  • cookies: Read your LinkedIn session cookie to verify you are logged in.
  • storage: Save connection status and apply results locally.
  • tabs: Open job application pages and navigate between them.
  • scripting: Inject form-filling scripts into ATS application pages.
  • Host permissions (LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, etc.): Access job listing and application pages to read job details and submit applications.

Data Retention

All data is stored locally in your browser. Uninstalling the Extension removes all stored data. No server-side data retention exists.

Third-Party Services

The Extension interacts with the following services only when you initiate a job application:

  • LinkedIn (linkedin.com) — job discovery and Easy Apply
  • ATS platforms (Greenhouse, Lever, Workable, Ashby, etc.) — external job applications

These interactions use your existing browser sessions. The Extension does not create accounts or authenticate with these services independently.
